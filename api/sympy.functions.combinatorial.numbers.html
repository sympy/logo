Module s.f.c.numbers

Part of sympy.functions.combinatorial

This module implements some special functions that commonly appear in combinatorial contexts (e.g. in power series); in particular, sequences of rational numbers such as Bernoulli and Fibonacci numbers.

Factorials, binomial coefficients and related functions are located in the separate 'factorials' module.
